ABOUT ME

I am a master's student in business informatics from Slovenia. I feel in love with blockchain technologies in 2019, when I first invested into Bitcoin and I have been learning about it ever since. I first started as an investor but with time started to understand blockchain and its concepts, so I wanted to make my own projects. I first started with Moralis Academy, back then it was Ivan On Tech Academy. Through those courses I developed understanding of Javascript and using Binance Apis to retreieve the data. For some time I was all in development of trading bots. I made a lot of strategies programatically but I just didn’t seem enough. That’s when I turned to solidity develpment. I searched all the internet where I could get the

most knowledge about blockchain development. I found this amazing 32-hour course taught by no other than Patrick Collins himself. Throughout the course, I developed strong understanding of solidity development and hardhat. From there I still wanted to get more knowledge so I started CryptoZombies course which I finished in no time. After I got all the knowledge I needed, I started doing my own projects. Here I am now, presenting my portoflio page with the work I have done...
